The Brain

The brain controls everything from breathing and heartbeat to thoughts and emotions. It contains billions of neurons that communicate via electrical signals.

Different regions of the brain specialize in tasks such as processing sensory input, coordinating movement, and managing memories. The brain communicates with the rest of the body through the spinal cord and nerves.

A varied diet, mental stimulation, physical exercise, and quality sleep help keep your brain sharp. Avoiding excessive alcohol or drugs is also crucial.
