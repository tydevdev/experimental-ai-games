The Heart

The heart is a muscular organ that pumps blood throughout the body, supplying oxygen and nutrients while removing carbon dioxide and wastes. It beats about 100,000 times every day.

The heart has four chambers: two atria and two ventricles. Blood flows through a precise sequence of contractions, controlled by electrical impulses, which keeps circulation going.

Regular exercise, a balanced diet, and not smoking are key to maintaining a strong heart. Managing stress and getting enough sleep also contribute to cardiovascular health.
