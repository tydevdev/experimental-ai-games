Digestive System

The digestive system breaks down food into nutrients that the body uses for energy, growth, and cell repair. It also expels waste products.

Food travels from the mouth through the esophagus to the stomach, where it mixes with digestive juices. It then enters the intestines where nutrients are absorbed into the bloodstream.

Eating a balanced diet rich in fiber, staying hydrated, and managing stress help keep the digestive system running smoothly.
